Great results obtained the INAOE in MICAI 2019
  • INAOE
  • 01 November, 2019

From October 28 to November 1, the 18th edition of the Mexican International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, a high-level event covering all areas of artificial intelligence, was held in the city of Xalapa, Veracruz. Researchers and students from INAOE's Computational Science Coordination had outstanding participation and won various awards and recognitions.

The first place for the best article of the conference was for the work titled "RGB-D Camera and 2D Laser Integration for Robot Navigation In Dynamic Environments", by Orlando Lara-Guzmán, Sergio A. Serrano, David Carrillo-Lopez and Dr. Luis Enrique Sucar.

The award for the best article of a student was for "A Fast and Robust Deep Learning Approach for Hand Object Grasping Confirmation", by Sebastián Salazar-Colores, Arquímides Méndez-Molina, David Carrillo-López, Esaú Escobar-Juárez, Eduardo F. Morales and Dr. Luis Enrique Sucar.

Finally, the award for best poster of the conference was for the work "Depth EStimation using Optical Flow and CNN for the NAO Robot", by Oswualdo Alquisiris Quecha, master's student of the Coordination of Computational Sciences, and Dr. José Martínez Carranza, who is master's thesis supervisor of Oswualdo.

Congratulations to you all!
